About Me

I am a software engineer at Microsoft IDC in India, collaborating with Amit Singh and Dr. Ravishankar Krishnaswamy . My expertise lies in billion-scale information retrieval, with a focus on rapid and efficient candidate selection. I am particularly passionate about graph dense retrieval techniques for Search and Recommendation Systems.

My interest lies in Large scale informatin retrieval, NLP, Machine Learning, Aritifical Intelligence, Internet of Things. I am very passionate about innovation and competitive programming.

I graduated from National Institute of Technology, Rourkela in 2020 with a major in Computer Science and Engineering.
